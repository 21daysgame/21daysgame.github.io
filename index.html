21 Days

21 Days is a simulation adventure game in which you can experience the life of syrian refugees. Main character is a syrian man named Mohammed Shenu who escaped from Syria to western europe. Becoming Mohammed Shenu for 21 days, you have to manage money, time, hunger and mental status. You may experience the life of refugees who keep their body and soul with only a faint hope.
From now on, you are Mohammed Shenu.
